Đáp án: Last night Alice had a dream about a dancing strawberry. It was big and red just like a normal strawberry, but it had long legs. It looked delicious and danced very well. Alice woke up and went to the kitchen. "Mom, we have to eat a whole box of strawberries for breakfast!" said Alice. "Why?" asked Mom. "Are strawberries your favorite fruit now?" "No," answered Alice. "I just want to dance." After Alice ate the whole box of strawberries, she danced around the room.
