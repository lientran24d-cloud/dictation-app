Unit 7: Sleepy Jay

Đáp án: Jay was very sleepy today. In the morning, he slept next to his breakfast. At school, Jay slept on top of his textbook and under the blackboard. On the way home, he fell asleep at the crosswalk. Jay slept at the theater, the restaurant, the bookshop, and at church. Now it is time for bed. Jay lies down with his pillow and blanket. "That is funny," Jay thinks. "I am not tired anymore."
